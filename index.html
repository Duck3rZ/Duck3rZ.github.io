Introducing Me!

Maggie is an award winning writer, mischievous and intelligent animated baby. Maggie is impressionable and easily influenced by what she sees around her. Maggie is particularly interested in music (being called the "voice of her generation"), film maker and testing her mental and physical agility.

Margaret "Maggie" Simpson is a fictional character in the animated television series The Simpsons. Maggie is the youngest child of Homer and Marge, and sister to Bart and Lisa. She is often seen sucking on her red pacifier and, when she walks, she trips over her clothing and falls on her face. Being an infant, she has not yet learned how to talk but makes a characteristic sucking noise with her pacifier, which has become synonymous with the character.
